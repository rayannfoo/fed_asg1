PROFILE

SEVENTEEN is a group with 13 members and 3 units coming together to form one team. Seventeen debuted in 2015 on May 26.
They took their first step into the music industry with their first mini album '17 CARAT'.
Seventeen consists of 3 different unit within the team (Hip-Hop team , Vocal team and Performance team).
The three teams participates in the overall album production, earning them the title of 'self-produced ideas' solidifying thier status in the music industry both in Korea and worldwide.
